If you've had an accident outside of these hours and your car isn't driveable, please call our 24-hour accident recovery line on {{ phoneNumbers.motorClaims24 }}.

Questions we'll ask

  • What is your car's registration number?
  • Where did the incident take place?
  • What are the names, addresses, phone numbers, and registration numbers of the people involved? This can be the other driver and includes witnesses.

Don't worry if you don't have all of this information, it's important to call us as soon as you're safe, that way we can guide you through the claims process and provide the necessary support.

Once your claim has been registered, we'll arrange the recovery of your car, the repair at one of our recommended garages, and give it a clean before returning it to you.
