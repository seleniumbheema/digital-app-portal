If you're involved in an accident, follow our simple steps below and remember S‑A‑M

S - Stay safe

First of all, don't panic. Turn off your engine and put the hazard lights on. Check if you or anyone else is hurt and if it is serious, call 999.

A - Ask for information

Once it's safe to do so, try to get as much information from the other driver as possible, including:

  • Phone no.
  • Name
  • Address
  • Registration no.

Taking photos of the scene, and collecting details from witnesses can make the claims process quicker. It would also be helpful to give the other driver our phone number.
